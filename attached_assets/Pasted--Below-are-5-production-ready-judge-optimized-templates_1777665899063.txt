# 1️⃣ **Dentists — Research Digest (10/10 GOLD STANDARD)**

```text

A 2,100-patient trial shows 3-month fluoride recall reduces caries recurrence by 38% vs 6-month cycles.
```

### Why this scores 10:

* Specificity → 2,100 patients, 38%
* Merchant fit → “your high-risk adult patients”
* Category voice → clinical, no hype
* Compulsion → curiosity + reciprocity
* CTA → low friction

# 🧠 INSTRUCTIONS FOR REPLIT AGENT (IMPORTANT)

Give this along with templates:

```text

   problem → proof → benchmark → action

2. Must include:
   - at least ONE number
   - merchant/customer name
   - trigger reason

3. Must include EXACTLY ONE CTA

4. Must include ONE compulsion:
   - urgency OR
   - social proof OR
   - loss OR
   - curiosity OR
   - reciprocity

5. Keep under 3–4 lines (WhatsApp style)

6. Never use generic phrases:
   - "boost sales"
   - "improve growth"
   - "let me know"

7. Prefer:
   "ready in 10 min" → reduces friction massively
```
